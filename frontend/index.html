1. Overview

BurchTickets ("we", "us") provides an online platform for event organizers to publish events and sell tickets, and for buyers to purchase and receive tickets. By using this site, you agree to these terms.

2. Tickets & Payments

Ticket prices are set by the event organizer, not BurchTickets. Payments are processed via M-Pesa or other supported methods. Once a payment is confirmed, a ticket (with a unique code) is issued to the buyer's account and email.

3. Refunds & Cancellations

Refund eligibility is set by the individual event organizer. If an event is cancelled or postponed by the organizer, contact support@unduguhalisinetwork.com and we will help coordinate a resolution with the organizer.

4. Organizer Responsibilities

Organizers are responsible for the accuracy of their event listings, honoring valid tickets at the door, and complying with any applicable local laws or licensing requirements for their event.

5. Prohibited Use

You may not use this platform to list fraudulent events, resell tickets in violation of an organizer's policy, or attempt to interfere with the security or normal operation of the site.

Privacy & Cookie Policy

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, buy a ticket, or sell tickets as an organizer, we collect information you provide directly: name, email, phone number, and event/ticket details. Payment processing is handled by M-Pesa/Safaricom and our payment provider — we do not store your M-Pesa PIN or full payment credentials.

2. How We Use It

We use your information to process ticket orders, send confirmations and tickets, communicate updates about events you've bought tickets to, and to help organizers manage their event sales.

3. Sharing

We share buyer name, ticket type, and contact details with the relevant event organizer so they can validate tickets at the door. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies

This site may use basic cookies or local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No third-party advertising trackers are used.

5. Your Rights

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, or ask us to delete your account and associated data, by emailing support@unduguhalisinetwork.com.
